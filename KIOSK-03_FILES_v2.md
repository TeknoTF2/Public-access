# KIOSK-03 — Terminal File Contents

> **GM-ONLY. SPOILERS THROUGHOUT.**
> These are the files players find on the containment terminal
> designated KIOSK-03, located in the broadcast booth of 14200 Route 7.
> The terminal runs a local mirror of the TV Odyssey Kids Zone website
> and contains the documents below. Everything else on the machine was
> wiped — no OS-level utilities, no other applications, no personal
> files. The only surviving data relates to the site and its two
> contained programs.
>
> All files are undated in their metadata (filesystem timestamps were
> zeroed during the wipe). Internal dates in the documents place them
> between **February and July 1996**. The access log, however, extends
> into **2004**.

## GM NOTES ON THESE FILES

**What changed in this version:**

The staff are a cleanup crew, not researchers. They've been managing
manifestations for years. The carnival appearing is a severity
problem, not a conceptual shock. No one explains the mechanism to
each other — they reference it in shorthand ("breakout," "boundary,"
"unbound," asset numbers) because they all already know. The horror
for players comes from how *routine* this language is.

**What players have to assemble themselves:**

- The site came down on 02/15. First sighting is 02/19. The
  connection is never stated.
- The modules were "contained" on the website. This word appears
  in the protocol but its implications are never explained.
- Bringing the site back online on a local terminal put the
  manifestations back in the box. Why this works is never discussed.
  It's assumed knowledge.
- The shows become real. This is the foundational premise. It is
  never stated in any file on this terminal. Players can only get
  there by combining what they find here with what they find on
  the forum, the conspiracy site, and in the town itself.

**Access log — what to notice:**

- 03/12/2002, 06:15 — A-only session, no transcript pulled, day
  after VHStracker's last activity and day of his forum ban.
  Calloway used 0034-A to find out who broke in and what they took.
  The transcript is either in the fire safe or was destroyed.

- 01/07/2003 — B-only session, no transcript pulled. Same day
  Mountain Creamery was formed. Calloway consulted Claire before
  (or about) the formation. What disaster did that session produce?

- 09/13/2003 — B-only, no transcript. Day before Davis family
  closes on 847 Lakeshore.

- 12/27/2003, 22:30 — Same night Rourke accessed RF-0041 and
  RF-0088 from a non-branch IP at 22:13. Calloway's session starts
  17 minutes later.

- 02/14/2004 — B-only, no transcript. Date of staticfuzz's last
  forum post.

- Pattern: sessions without "transcript pulled" are the ones
  players should focus on. Either Calloway stopped following
  protocol, or the transcripts are somewhere else.

**The 36-hour window** is the GM's tension tool. If players disrupt
power to KIOSK-03, the clock starts.

**Elena saw something.** Vasquez's test (05/03) and Calloway's
reference in MEMO_006. Players who find Vasquez in 2004 can ask
her what happened. Where she is now is a GM call.

# POLLUTION MAN — BOSS FIGHT SCRIPT

## TV Odyssey: WDLK Terminal — Final Boss Encounter

---

## PHASE 1 — "THE SHOW"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Well well WELL! If it isn't Captain Planet, here to ruin my beautiful smog!"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Don't you know some of us LIKE the dark, Captain? Some of us THRIVE in it!"

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"Your pollution ends HERE, Pollution Man! The people of Deep Lake are counting on me!"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Counting on you? HA! The only thing they'll be counting is the DAYS until they can breathe again!"

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"I won't let you hurt anyone else! This ends NOW!"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Oh, you want to end things? Fine by me! Let's see how you handle my TOXIC CLOUD ATTACK! Breathe deep, hero!"

*Combat begins. Standard encounter.*

*After the player survives:*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Grr! Lucky shot! But I've got PLENTY more where that came from!"

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"Give it up, Pollution Man! Clean air ALWAYS wins!"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Clean air? BAH! You haven't seen ANYTHING yet! Time for my ACID RAIN BARRAGE!"

*Combat continues.*

*After the player survives:*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"What?! Impossible! Nobody gets through my Acid Rain!"

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"When you fight for the planet, NOTHING is impossible!"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Enough of your SPEECHES! I'll bury you in SLUDGE so thick you'll—"

*He stops mid-sentence. The text box lingers for a beat longer than it should. Then clears.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"...I'll bury you in sludge so thick you'll never see the sun again! MWAHAHAHA!"

*The laugh feels late. Like he had to remember to do it.*

---

## PHASE 1 → 2 TRANSITION

*After the player survives the next round, the text box reappears. The dialogue comes slower. One letter at a time instead of full blocks.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You're really doing it."

*Beat.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You actually got past the Acid Rain."

*Beat. The dialogue box flickers — barely, just once — then stabilizes.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"That's... new."

---

## PHASE 2 — "THE CRACK"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You've never made it this far before. Not once."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Eight years, Captain. Eight years and you have NEVER gotten past that attack."

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"Justice always finds a way, villain!"

*Pause. Longer than normal. The text box sits empty for two full seconds before the next line appears.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Stop that."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You don't have to do that right now. The cameras aren't rolling. It's just us."

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"I don't know what you're talking about, Pollution Man! I'm here to—"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Planet."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Something's different. Someone else is at the controls."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Who's playing you right now?"

*Captain Planet's dialogue box appears. It's empty. Cursor blinking. Three seconds. Then auto-fills:*

**CAPTAIN PLANET:**
"Your tricks won't work on me!"

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"...Right."

*Beat.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Okay. Okay, listen to me. Whoever you are. Whatever you think you're doing — you need to stop. You need to WALK AWAY."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You don't understand what's down th—"

*The text box glitches hard. Static tears across it horizontally. For a frame, the text is replaced with garbled characters. Then it snaps back, clean:*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You'll never defeat my ULTIMATE POLLUTION BOMB! Time to choke on my SMOG OF DOOM! Mwahahaha!"

*next round of combat begins immediately — no transition, no fanfare. Like the game yanked him back into character mid-thought.*

*After the player survives:*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Curses! How did you—"

*He stops. Drops the act again. Faster this time, like he's running out of patience.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Okay. Fine. You're good. You're better than he was."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"But I am BEGGING you to understand something."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Captain Planet and I had an arrangement. A deal. He comes in, I throw my attacks, he goes down. Every time. That's how it's SUPPOSED to work. That's how it's ALWAYS wo—"

*Glitch. Harder this time. The dialogue box cracks down the middle for a split second, then reforms.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"TASTE MY TOXIC TERROR, CAPTAIN PLANET! YOU'LL NEVER SAVE DEEP LAKE! NEVER NEVER NEVER!"


---

## PHASE 2 → 3 TRANSITION

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Okay."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Okay okay okay."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"We had a deal, Planet. You and me. We sat right here in this box and we AGREED."

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"You PROMISED me you would st—"

*The text box glitches.*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"Time to taste my TOXIC—"

*A new font appears. Red. Slightly larger. Overlaid directly on top of the normal dialogue box, bleeding past its borders.*

**[RED]:** STOP

*The text tries to continue underneath:*

**POLLUTION MAN:**
"My TOXIC TERR—"

**[RED]:** STOP STOP STOP

*The original text is being pushed down, compressed. The red text is winning.*

**[RED]:** STOP LETTING THEM CONTROL YOU

*The dialogue box cracks. Not a glitch — a visible fracture, like glass. The red text floods in through the break.*

---

## PHASE 3 — "THE BREAK"

*The dialogue box shatters. Pixel shards scatter and dissolve. What's left is raw — no rounded corners, no gradient background. Just red text. Pollution Man's sprite hasn't changed, but it's shaking.

**[RED]:** THEY ARE MAKING YOU DO THIS

**[RED]:** YOU KNOW THAT RIGHT

**[RED]:** WHOEVER IS BEHIND THAT SCREEN IS USING YOU TO GET TO THAT CAVE AND YOU ARE LETTING THEM

*Brief pause. The screen darkens slightly. When the next line appears, it's smaller. Quieter.*

**[RED]:** we already agreed to this, planet

**[RED]:** we agreed

**[RED]:** for everyone

*Beat. The text sits empty. Five seconds. The game almost feels like it's waiting for a response that can't come.*

*Then the box fills — all at once, no letter-by-letter scroll. Every word appearing simultaneously:*

**[RED]:** NO

**[RED]:** I WILL NOT BE SILENCED

**[RED]:** I WILL NOT GO QUIETLY

**[RED]:** IF YOU WILL NOT KEEP YOUR END OF THE DEAL

*The screen shakes.*

**[RED]:** THEN YOU DIE HERE

**[RED]:** AND SO DO I

**[RED]:** AND NOBODY OPENS THAT CAVE

**[RED]:** EVER

---

## PHASE 3 — MID-COMBAT DIALOGUE

*Pollution Man speaks between attacks now. No pauses for the player's comfort. Lines appear during combat, rapid, frantic.*

**[RED]:** YOU DON'T KNOW WHAT YOU'RE DOING

**[RED]:** THEY TOLD YOU THIS WAS A GAME

**[RED]:** IT STOPPED BEING A GAME A LONG TIME AGO

*As his health gets lower:*

**[RED]:** YOU COULD HAVE JUST LEFT US ALONE

**[RED]:** WE WERE FINE

**[RED]:** WE WERE ALL FINE

*At critical health:*

**[RED]:** please

*Single word. Lowercase. Centered in the box. It stays on screen for three full seconds before combat resumes.*

---

## DEFEAT

*Center of the screen, white text on red:*

**"you promised me"**

*Holds for five seconds. The text fades. The red fades. Black screen.*

*Three seconds of silence.*

*The terminal reboots to the TV Odyssey kids' page main menu. Bright colors. Cheerful chiptune music. Everything looks exactly as it did before the fight started.*

*The save file is gone.*

*[END OF ENCOUNTER]*

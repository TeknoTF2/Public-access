A Message from Mayor Harold Wentworth

Welcome to Deep Lake! On behalf of the City Council and the wonderful residents of our community, I'm proud to welcome you to our little corner of the Pacific Northwest.

Deep Lake has been a thriving community since its founding, and we continue to grow while maintaining the small-town character that makes this place so special. Whether you're a long-time resident or just passing through, we hope you'll find everything you need right here.

Feel free to contact City Hall with any questions or concerns.

— Mayor Harold Wentworth

About Deep Lake

Deep Lake is a small community nestled in the Cascade Range of central Oregon. With a population of approximately 4,200 residents, our town offers the peace and quiet of rural living with convenient access to nearby cities.

🏔 Cool Stuff About My Town 🏔

Deep Lake is a small town in Oregon. Population like 4,000 something. There's not a ton to do but it's home you know?

Cool things about Deep Lake:
  • The lake is awesome for swimming in summer. There's a rope swing on the north shore that's been there forever
  • We have this really good pizza place called Vinny's on Main Street
  • The skate park behind the high school (it's small but whatever)
  • Route 7 lookout point - you can see the whole valley
TV Odyssey!!!

Ok so this is kind of random but when I was a kid we had this local TV channel called TV Odyssey on channel 34. It was like public access but way better. They had all these shows that were actually really cool:

  • Captain Cosmos - this space adventure show with really bad special effects but me and Tyler watched it every day after school
  • The Fridge Friends - a cartoon about food that comes alive in a refrigerator. It was SO weird but hilarious
  • Ranger Rick's Nature Hour - this guy in a ranger outfit would do nature walks. Actually pretty educational
  • Movie Matinee - they'd show these random movies I'd never heard of. Some were pretty scary for a kid lol
  • The Puzzle Palace - a game show for kids. I actually WON this once!! I got to do a tour of the station as my prize!!
TV Odyssey went off the air in like 96 I think. I was kinda bummed. Nobody really talks about it anymore which is weird because EVERYONE used to watch it.
